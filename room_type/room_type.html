Duplex Room

The most expensive room provided by a hotel.
Usually, only one suplex suite is available in one single hotel property.
Similar to the normal suites, a Duplex suite always has one or more bedrooms and
a living space with a string emphasis on grand in - room decoration,
high - quality amenities and supplies and tailor - made services
(e.g. personal butler during the stay).
